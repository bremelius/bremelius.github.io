About Me

I'm Ben Remelius, a marketing leader and storyteller in the world of sports. From collegiate athletics to summer league baseball, I’ve helped organizations grow attendance, merchandise sales, and community visibility. Currently, I serve as Assistant Athletic Director for Marketing and Promotions at the University of Central Missouri, where I lead strategy for branding, digital media, and fan engagement across 17 varsity sports. I also founded You Might Know Sports, a digital marketing and analytics consultancy, and have created content for teams across the country. I’m passionate about creative solutions that blend data with storytelling.

Education

I graduated in May of 2023 with my Bachelor of Science in Statistics from the University of Missouri.

I am currently pursuing a Master of Business Administration, also from the University of Missouri, with an expected date of graduation of December 2026.
