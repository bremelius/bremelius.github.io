Assistant Athletic Director, Marketing and Promotions

I'm Ben Remelius, a marketing leader and storyteller in the world of sports. From collegiate athletics to summer league baseball, I’ve helped organizations grow attendance, merchandise sales, and community visibility.

I also founded You Might Know Sports, a digital marketing and analytics consultancy, and have created content for teams across the country. I’m passionate about creative solutions that blend data with storytelling.
